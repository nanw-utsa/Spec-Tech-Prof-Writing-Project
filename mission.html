Mission Statement

Our team at Sipra seeks to provide consumers with a water bottle that never sacrifices sustainability and customer health in favor of greenwashed promises that sound good but fail to deliver. We seek to advocate awareness about the health risks associated with current watter bottle trends--be they disposable or reusable--all while representing oft-ignored consumer interests.

We hope that Sipra can inspire a new generation of environmentally-conscious and health-minded water drinkers, and we encourage other water bottle manufacturers to follow our lead in products that truly put the customer first.
