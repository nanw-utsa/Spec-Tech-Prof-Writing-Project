Features

Sipra is a high quality, solution-oriented, sustainable water bottle brand that leverages emerging technologies and sustainable design elements.

Borosilicate glass

This heat-resistant and biodegradable type of glass does not contain microplastics or require potentially risky liner materials. Our bottles have undergone rigorous stress and drop tests to ensure resistance to breaking. In the rare case that a Sipra bottle breaks, the glass is designed to break cleanly rather than completely shatter, making cleanup easy no matter what.

Cork cover and leak proofing

Not only does Sipra's use of cork lend to an effortlessly classy, vintage look, it offers significant improvements over other water bottle designs. Instead of bacteria-prone and environmentally-harmful silicone parts, Sipra uses biodegradable components to protect and leakproof bottles.

Artisan stamping

Glass and cork materials allow for unique and stylish branding; our logo is heat-stamped into the cork lid and meticulously etched into the glass' surface. This style looks especially beautiful when accompanied with decorative stickers.

Removable and replaceable components

All Sipra components can be easily removed for washing, and can be purchased individually from our storefront for an affordable price.

Diverse models

In order to address multiple use cases and accessibility issues, Sipra offers a variety of lids, extra components like infuser cores, and two different bottle types: a standard shape for on-the-go sipping and smaller bottles for mothers of young children. For customers with unique needs, Sipra has you covered.

Prioritizing accountability and customer agency

At Sipra, we prioritize giving customers all the information they need to make informed choices about how to stay hydrated. Not only does release yearly budget reports to the public to maintain accountability, our design team is staying constantly up-to-date on the latest technologies and communicating updates through our newsletter, ensuring that customers are always informed about the latest ways to drink safely and sustainably.

Bacteria risk calculator

For those of us who care about their environmental footprint, moving from single-use plastic water bottles to reusable plastic or metal water bottles is a big step in the right direction. Customers often assume these water bottles are safe, but many carry risks of bacterial growth. Design elements are frequently difficult to clean, especially flexible silicone components, which are often the most prone to bacterial infiltration.
